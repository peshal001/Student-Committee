Membership

Join our student Committee to enjoy a wide range of benefits and opportunities to enhance your university experience.

  • Premium Membership: All Basic Membership benefits plus exclusive workshops, priority event registration, special discounts, and automatic registration to all events throughout the subscription year.

Benefits

  • Networking opportunities with peers and professionals

Student Committee Agreement

By joining the student committee membership, you agree to abide by the rules and regulations set forth by the committee, which includes active participation in meetings, adherence to the code of conduct, timely payment of any dues, attendance and contribution to committee-organized events, regular communication and responsiveness, and a commitment to volunteer for various roles and responsibilities within the committee, understanding that failure to comply may result in the termination of your membership.
